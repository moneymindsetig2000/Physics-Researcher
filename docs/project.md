# Physica AI

## Project Overview

Physica AI is an AI-powered research platform built specifically for physics researchers, professors, PhD students, masters students, research groups, academic institutions, and anyone working with scientific literature.

The platform is designed to help users discover, understand, analyze, summarize, and explore research papers significantly faster than traditional academic workflows.

Rather than functioning as a general-purpose chatbot, Physica AI acts as a specialized research partner focused on physics and related scientific disciplines.

The primary objective is to reduce the time researchers spend searching through papers and increase the time they spend understanding, learning, and producing research.

---

# Vision

Modern researchers face an overwhelming amount of scientific literature.

Thousands of new papers are published every month across multiple repositories and disciplines.

Finding relevant work, understanding technical content, comparing approaches, extracting insights, and identifying research gaps often requires hours or days of manual effort.

Physica AI aims to become the intelligent research companion that assists researchers throughout the entire paper exploration process.

The long-term vision is to create the most advanced AI-native platform for scientific research discovery and understanding.

# Research Sources

The platform focuses on publicly accessible scientific repositories and research sources.

Examples include:

* arXiv
* INSPIRE
* NASA ADS
* Open-access scientific repositories

The objective is to provide access to research knowledge without maintaining a massive proprietary paper database.

---

# Search Philosophy

The platform should prioritize repository-based search before broader web search.

Workflow:

1. Understand user intent.
2. Search scientific repositories.
3. Rank relevant results.
4. Retrieve relevant papers.
5. Analyze content.
6. Present findings.

General web search should be used only when repository search cannot confidently resolve a concept, researcher, historical reference, or ambiguous query.

---

# AI Philosophy

AI should function as:

* Research assistant
* Knowledge navigator
* Scientific guide

AI should not:

* Fabricate citations
* Invent research findings
* Create false references
* Misrepresent scientific claims

Transparency and accuracy are critical.
